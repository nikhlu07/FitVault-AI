# FitVault-AI: Autonomous Health Commitment Infrastructure

**Powered by Tether. Driven by Sweat. Secured by Smart Contracts.**

---

## 📉 The Problem: Legacy Move-to-Earn

The first generation of Move-to-Earn (M2E) protocols like StepN and Sweatcoin shared an elegant premise and a **fatal design flaw**.

### The Death Spiral
They rewarded activity with project-native tokens, creating an immediate supply-demand imbalance:
1. **Tokens Minted** continuously as rewards.
2. **Value Relies** entirely on new users buying in.
3. **Growth Stalls**, token value collapses.
4. **Incentive to move vanishes.**

> Any system that pays rewards by printing its own currency is structurally unsustainable.

---

## 💡 The Solution: Commitment-as-a-Service

**FitVault-AI does not print tokens.** We invert the model.

- **Real Assets:** Users stake assets they already own (USD₮) against a personal fitness goal.
- **Accountability Layer:** The protocol acts as an impartial, autonomous referee.
- **Real Yield:** The protocol deploys the staked capital into established DeFi protocols (e.g. Aave V3) to generate real yield.

Succeed, and you reclaim your principal plus 85% of everything it earned. Miss your goal, and the forfeit flows to a community reward pool.

---

## 🔄 How it Works (The Loop)

1. **Stake:** Lock USD₮ into a non-custodial vault and define a biometric goal (e.g., 10k steps/day).
2. **Deploy:** Capital immediately deployed to Aave V3 for yield.
3. **Monitor:** OpenClaw AI agent tracks health oracle data autonomously.
4. **Settle:** 
   - **Goal Met:** User reclaims Principal + 85% Yield.
   - **Goal Missed:** Forfeit 5% to Protocol, 95% to Community Pool. No appeals.

---

## 🏗️ System Architecture

FitVault-AI separates reasoning (AI) from execution (Finance).

### 🧠 The Brain (OpenClaw Agent)
- **Normalizes Data:** Converts raw oracle feeds (heart rate, steps) into objective scores.
- **Plans Finances:** Rebalances DeFi positions based on rates.
- **Executes Autonomously:** Strictly follows vault logic without manual intervention.

### 🤝 The Hands (Tether WDK)
- **Non-Custodial:** BIP-44 HD Wallets (never holds your keys).
- **DeFi Execution:** Aave V3 for lending, Paraswap for swapping.
- **Multi-Chain:** Ethereum, Polygon, Solana.

## 💰 Business Model: Sustainable Revenue

We profit when you win.

1. **Yield Share (15%)**
   The protocol retains 15% of the DeFi yield generated on staked assets when a user succeeds.
2. **Forfeit Fees (5%)**
   A modest management fee on failed vaults before the rest flows to the community pool.
3. **AaaS Subscriptions**
   Micro-fees for users requiring high-frequency monitoring (e.g., hourly compliance checks).

FitVault-AI generates revenue from genuine economic activity, not from venture subsidies or token issuance.
